# College Application Tracking

1. Curated List of Colleges: Based on a student's academic credits, extra curricular activities, projects, research, internships and preferred destinations for higher studies, the counsellor curates a list of colleges and group them into Dream, Reach and Match.
2. Final List of Colleges: Student and their Parents review the curated list of colleges and filter the list based on following parameters: interested major, college rankings for that major, cost of attendance. A typical student has 10 to 15 colleges, sometimes going upto 20 colleges. 
3. Destinations: Historically, 90% of the student population apply to US colleges. Many of the students are US passport holders, living in India or USA. With recent restrictions in US student visa applications, other destinations like Canada, UK, Australia, Singapore, EU and Singapore have become very popular.
